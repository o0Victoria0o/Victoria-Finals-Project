Hakone Turnpike (箱根ターンパイク)

A broad, high-horsepower toll road featuring expansive sweeping turns and a notorious high-altitude "Death Zone" blanketed in heavy fog. Defending these sweeping bends forces machinery and drivers to hold wide-open, nerve-wracking throttle positions.

Downhill Record: 3:56.188 (Takumi Fujiwara)

Uphill Record: 3:56.600 (Keisuke Takahashi)
