Irohazaka (いろは坂)

A strictly one-way downward slope in Tochigi featuring a punishing rhythm of short straights punctuated by immediate, severe hairpin turns. Standard cornering speeds fail here, forcing drivers to invent gravity-defying lines over the curbs to survive.

Downhill Record: 3:50.939 (Takumi Fujiwara)

Uphill Record: 3:59.386 (Keisuke Takahashi)
